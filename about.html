About Artistica Gallery

At Artistica Gallery we strive to bring our customers nothing but the finest art that Michigan has to offer.

Artistica Gallery is a unique shop featuring art and fine gifts created by local and regional artists. Think of us when you are searching for that special gift for any occation or maybe just something special for yourself. Look for the red door by the village clock in charming downtown Dexter.
